ABOUT

OUR INSTITUTION

Prospect IAS is an institution of committed academicians, CSE experts and established research scholars drawn from diverse fields of knowledge. We aspire to ingrain comprehensive knowledge base with critical thinking and analytical abilities in the aspirants of Civil Services Examination. We strive for focused, coordinated and up-to-date approach to crack Civil Services Examination. Prospect IAS believes in inculcation of comprehensive knowledge of the concepts along with exam- oriented understanding of disciplines, which can be achieved only with the guidance of a diverse team of learned academicians, passionate teachers and experienced bureaucrats. Prospect IAS mentors the journey of CSE aspirants and guides them through the path of self-development.

Popular Courses

CLASSROOM COURSES

Prospect IAS GS Advance and Foundation courses are exclusively designed for comprehensive coverage of both Preliminary and Mains syllabus of UPSC Civil Services Examination. The course will be taken by established faculty and subject experts of Prospect IAS.

ONLINE/LIVE CLASSES

Online/Live classes are becoming an important part of human existence. Due to professional job locations, college timings, and high living costs in metropolitan cities, it is difficult for many aspirants to attend offline classes. Prospect IAS offers online classes to make their preparation journey easier.

TEST SERIES (Offline and Online)

Prospect IAS offers GS Prelims and Main Test Series simulating real UPSC examination experience. The experience acquaints the aspirants with authentic challenges faced during the exam, helping them to prepare and strategize better.

Interview Guidance

After successfully qualifying Main examination, interview remains the last barrier between the aspirant and the highly reputed civil services. Prospect IAS engages bureaucrats, esteemed academicians and CSE experts to help you out to win the last battle. An exclusive batch for Interview Guidance starts immediately after the declaration of the Main Examination results.
